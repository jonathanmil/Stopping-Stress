Methods to reducing stress for me

Working out both releases anger and also makes me stronger. I have always wanted to be stronger and working out is a clear example of how I am able to achieve this goal.


  • A clear example of something that I use everyday in order to reduce is running up and down the stairs. Without being able to go outside this simple type of cardio is something I have started using everyday. It is far more useful then ever expected and I continute to use it.
